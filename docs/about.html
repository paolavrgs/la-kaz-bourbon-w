About Us

Discover with us the wonders of Réunion, the ultimate island

A couple of world traveler with an insatiable passion for discovering this magnificent planet, we love meeting new people from all over the globe. During our travels, we never forget to take the time to appreciate the small pleasures in life... no matter how far from home we may be.

Along the way, we've learned that feeling welcome and comfortable is an absolute must in every journey we take. This is why we make every effort to ensure that your visit to La Réunion will be like coming home. Home to the paradise we live in, love, and love to share! The ultimate island.
